Risk:

Privacy Risk

If identity isn’t verified properly, an unauthorised person could access personal information, leading to a privacy breach and misuse of the information.

Fraud Risk

If identity checks fail, someone could pretend to be the customer and make changes or requests that lead to fraud.

Reputational Risk

A failed identity check could lead to customer trust issues and damage the company’s reputation.


Controls:

Privacy Risk.

Customer identity must be verified using standard verification questions before any information is shared. No information is provided unless verification is completed successfully.

Fraud Risk

No changes or actions can be made on an account until identity verification is completed successfully

Reputational Risk

Staff follow the identity verification process and document all interactions clearly to create an audit trail.
